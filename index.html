How Creda tag works

Three simple steps to make your money transfers easier than ever

Create You Tag

Sign in to your Creda app and create a unique name using your email address which becomes your Creda Tag.

Share You Tag

Let friends and family know your Creda Tag for hassle-free transfers. You can search other recipients via their tag too.

Send & Receive Instantly

Funds arrive in your account immediately, no matter how they're sent.

Creda Tag is completely optional, you can choose to use the normal way.
